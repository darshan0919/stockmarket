# Catalyst Taxonomy — what counts as "significant"

Design principle: only **structural** news that can shift business perception
for multiple weeks. Severity HIGH = notify, MEDIUM = digest, RISK = negative
structural flag, suppressed = routine compliance.

## 1. ORDER WIN
Triggers: Award/Receipt of Order, LOI/LOA, work/purchase order, contract win,
export order, repeat order.
- HIGH: value/TTM revenue >= 0.30x (user bar) OR marquee counterparty.
- MEDIUM: 0.10–0.30x, trending-theme order, or undisclosed value (size it from PDF).
- Suppressed: disclosed value < 0.10x with no marquee/theme.
Marquee = MAG7 + hyperscalers, global semis/industrial/defence/energy/pharma
majors, Indian anchors (Railways, DRDO/MoD, HAL/BEL, NTPC/PGCIL, Reliance/Jio,
Tata, Adani, L&T, BSNL/BharatNet...). Themes = AI/data centre, semis/OSAT,
nuclear/SMR, green hydrogen, BESS, solar cell, drones/anti-drone, Kavach,
missiles/radar/satellite, 5G/6G, HVDC/transformers, EV, defence exports.

## 2. CAPITAL RAISE / SMART MONEY
- Preferential issue / warrants / QIP / rights: MEDIUM default; HIGH when
  promoter participates or marquee allottee named. Warrant CONVERSION (balance
  75% paid) = money actually in = conviction signal, kept.
- SAST Reg 29(1)/(2)/31 + PIT Reg 7(2): HIGH for marquee investors
  (Kacholia, Kedia, Kela, Agrawal, Abakkus, WhiteOak, big MFs/FPIs, SWFs...);
  MEDIUM for any institutional entity (parsed from "...for <entity>");
  individuals suppressed. Direction is never in the summary — verify in PDF.

## 3. EARNINGS
Result filings in window: MEDIUM review alert; HIGH when text itself cites
>= 50% growth. Clean-vs-reported verification belongs to
quarterly-result-analysis, not this scanner.

## 4. DELEVERAGING / RATINGS
- Credit rating UPGRADE: HIGH (independent balance-sheet validation; cuts cost
  of capital). Outlook-positive revision: MEDIUM. Reaffirmation: suppressed.
- Debt prepayment / NCD redemption / debt-free declaration: HIGH.
- Promoter pledge RELEASE: HIGH (classic re-rating precursor).

## 5. CAPACITY / APPROVALS
- Commissioning / commercial production / expansion / greenfield: MEDIUM;
  HIGH when capex >= 15% TTM revenue or trending theme.
- USFDA EIR/ANDA, EU-GMP/WHO-GMP, PLI approval, patent grant, BIS/PESO/DGCA:
  HIGH (market/incentive unlock, multi-quarter relevance).

## 6. M&A / STRATEGIC
Acquisition, demerger, scheme of arrangement, slump sale, JV, technology
tie-up, MoU (word-boundary matched!), open offer, delisting, diversification.
- HIGH: marquee counterparty or value >= 15% TTM revenue.
- MEDIUM: otherwise.
- Suppressed: investments into wholly-owned subsidiaries with no disclosed
  value (routine intra-group funding).
- Buyback: always HIGH (size vs float and acceptance ratio to be checked).

## 7. PRICE / VOLUME (scan-column channel)
- 1D >= +7% or 1W >= +12%: MEDIUM momentum alert with CRS context.
- Exchange "clarification regarding price/volume movement": MEDIUM meta-signal
  (the market is moving on something).

## 8. INSTITUTIONAL INTEREST (meta)
Plant/site visits by institutional investors: MEDIUM (buy-side diligence
footprint, often precedes position-building — the HFCL/MTAR pattern).
Routine earnings-call intimations: suppressed.

## 9. RISK channel (forensic lens — always on)
Pledge creation/encumbrance, auditor or CFO/MD resignation, IT/GST/ED raids,
SEBI orders, defaults/NCLT, plant fire/accident, trading suspension, rating
downgrade. Surfaced as RISK regardless of "catalyst" framing — structural
negatives are also multi-week perception events.

## Other investor-catalyst classes encoded (from practitioner playbooks)
- First-ever concall / investor PPT / analyst meet after years of silence
  (the inverse of the "no investor communications" governance flag).
- Creeping promoter acquisition (Reg 31 increases without preferential).
- Anti-dumping duty / policy wins, index inclusion language.
- Subsidiary value-unlocking (demerger/IPO language) — caught by M&A patterns.

## Noise (suppressed)
AGM/EGM, book closure, record date, trading window, newspaper ads, share
certificate matters, investor grievance, Reg 74(5), Reg 57 NCD interest
certificates, RTA changes, postal ballot, e-voting, routine ESOP allotments,
board-meeting intimations without outcomes, secretarial compliance,
insurance claim receipts, share-capital reconciliation.

## Confluence escalation (orchestrator-level)
>= 3 distinct structural filings (non-meta, non-RISK categories) by one
company within the scan window escalates its MEDIUM structural alerts to HIGH
with an explicit CONFLUENCE note — multiple simultaneous Reg-30 filings are
the signature of an orchestrated strategic event (validated on HFCL's
2026-06-03 HASPL defence restructuring: 4 filings, ~Rs 175 cr cluster,
Rs 1,890 cr export order book, +4.5% intraday reaction).
